My Research

What I Do

My research program centers on applications to personnel selection and applied psychometrics, including testing, assessment, and measurement, especially related to personality. Specifically, there is a focus on the measurement of implicit personality via conditional reasoning. as well as various questions related to this measurement system. Additionally, there exists a broad interest in research methodology, both in terms of advancing my own expertise using various methods (e.g. MLM, LPA, Meta-Analysis), and also improving/building upon these same methods to advance our understanding of I-O and management relevant phenomena.

Testing & Assessment

How can we deter and/or detect faking on selection assessments? Are some assessments resistant to faking? Which assessments are appropriate for cross-cultural use?

Personality

How do implicit and explicit personality interact to uniquely predict work-related outcomes? Can we further validate the use of established conditional reasoning tests as well as develop new tests?

Research Methods

How can person centered methods be used to answer I-O and management relevant questions? How can we improve statistical power in tests of moderation? Does cross-level bias exist in how we assess scholarly impact?
